Topic 1 - Macroeconomic Indicators
- provide an idea of overall economic health.

Gross Domestic Product
- final goods and services
-- computed using market value
--- (M = P X Q)
- no intermediate goods
-- only current productions
-- no transfer payments
--- no non-current period of goods

Approaches in Measuring GDP
- Expenditure approach
-- GDP = C + I + G + NX
--- Listed are consumption, investment, government spending, and net exports

- Income approach
-- GDP = COE + GOS + IBTS
--- Listed are compensation of employees, gross operating surplus, and indirect tax less subsidies

- Value-added approach
-- GRP = Va + Vi + Vs
--- Listed are agriculture + fishery + forestry, industry and services
