Axioms of rational choice
- completeness / comparability
-- consumers can rank bundles
--- A > B or A ≻ B
--- A < B or B ≻ A
--- A = B or A ~ B
- transitivity of preference
-- given bundles A, B and C
--- if A ≻ B and B ≻ C, then A ≻ C
--- if A ~ B and B ~ C, then A ~ C
- convexity of preferences
-- average is preferred over extremes
- non-satiation
-- more is preffered to less
- continuity
-- preferences carry to similar situations
